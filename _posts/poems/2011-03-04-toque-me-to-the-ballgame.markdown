date: 2011-03-04 05:10

i must buy a toque,
it's winter now.
i know what you're thinking,
"isn't it always winter in canada?!"
yes, this is true. 
it is always winter here.
but we have warm winters and cold winters.
the cold one is now upon us.

i must buy a toque,
it's cold winter now.
